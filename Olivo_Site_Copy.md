## PAGE 6: ABOUT

---

*[PHOTO CUE: Atmospheric dining room shot — warm lighting, wine wall]*

**Headline:** A New Chapter for a Beloved Address

**Body:**

For nearly four decades, the address at 58 US-46 in Pine Brook was synonymous with one of New Jersey's most iconic dining institutions — Don Pepe's Steakhouse, a beloved landmark known for its prime cuts, Spanish flavors, and the kind of warm, family-owned hospitality that kept guests coming back for generations.

Today, that same address is home to something new — and something deeply familiar.

Olivo Mediterranean Fare is a reimagining of that storied space, honoring the legacy of what came before while opening the door to a broader, richer expression of Mediterranean cuisine. The dry-aged steaks that made this address famous are still here. So is the Spanish soul. But now, the table is set wider — with handcrafted paellas, fresh seafood, curated tapas boards, and cocktails that carry you to the coast of Barcelona or the islands of Greece.

The dining room has been revitalized with a sleek, modern aesthetic — a curated wine wall, warm lighting, and an atmosphere that feels as right for a weeknight dinner as it does for a special celebration.

At Olivo, the tradition runs deep. The welcome is genuine. And the food speaks for itself.
