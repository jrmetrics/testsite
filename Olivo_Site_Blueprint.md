- **Headings:** Elegant serif or refined script (to echo the logo's hand-lettered "Olivo")

### Style
Modern and minimal. Generous white space. High-impact food photography. The design should feel upscale but welcoming — not stiff.

## Page-by-Page Content Plan

---

### 1. Home

**Goal:** Immediately communicate what Olivo is and drive visitors to reserve or order.

**Sections:**
- **Hero:** Full-bleed food or dining room photo. Headline: *"Mediterranean Flavors. Spanish Soul."* CTAs: [Reserve a Table] [Order Online]
- **Intro strip:** One or two sentences about the restaurant. e.g., *"Olivo brings the warmth of the Mediterranean to Pine Brook — handcrafted paellas, wood-fired meats, and tapas meant for sharing."*
- **Photo gallery row:** 3–4 signature dish images (paella, grilled octopus, cocktails)
- **Menu teaser:** Brief section with a [View Menu] button
- **Social proof:** Pull quote from a standout Google review (4.9 ★ / 2,350 reviews) + link
- **Events callout:** Short section — *"Private Dining & Events"* with a [Learn More] button
- **Instagram feed:** @olivorestaurant (Squarespace has a native Instagram block)
- **Footer:** Hours, address, phone, nav links, social icons

---

### 2. Menu

**Goal:** Let guests browse the full menu before they arrive.

**Structure:**
- Use Squarespace's **Menu block** (native restaurant feature)
- Sections: Tapas / Small Plates, Paellas, Entrees, Desserts, Cocktails, Wine & Beer
- Include item names, descriptions, and prices
- Note seasonal or chef's special items

*Content needed from client: full current menu with descriptions and prices.*

---

### 3. Reservations

**Goal:** Drive bookings through OpenTable.

**Approach:**
- Embed the **OpenTable reservation widget** (Squarespace supports this natively)
- Add a short line of copy above the widget: *"Join us for lunch or dinner. Reservations are recommended, especially on weekends."*
- Note hours and walk-in policy

---

### 4. Order Online

**Goal:** Make it easy to order takeout/delivery.

**Approach:**
- If using a third-party service (e.g., Toast, Tock, or DoorDash), embed or link directly
- Keep the page clean: short intro + prominent [Order Now] button
- Option to mention curbside pickup or any ordering notes

*Content needed: which ordering platform does Olivo use?*

---

### 5. Events & Private Dining

**Goal:** Capture event inquiries.

**Sections:**
- Description of private dining room and event capabilities
- Mention capacity, occasion types (corporate, birthdays, weddings, etc.)
- 1–2 photos of the private dining room
- **Inquiry form** (Squarespace form block): Name, Email, Phone, Event Date, Guest Count, Notes
- Note: Squarespace will email form submissions directly to the restaurant

## Squarespace-Specific Recommendations

### Template
Look for templates in the **"Restaurants & Food"** category, specifically those with:
- Full-bleed cover pages
- Menu block support
- Clean, minimal layout
- Good mobile responsiveness

Top candidates to evaluate: **Brine**, **Nolan**, **Galapagos**, or any fluid-engine template (Squarespace 7.1 is recommended over 7.0 for flexibility).

### Key Native Features to Use
- **Menu Blocks** — for the full menu
- **OpenTable block** — for reservations
- **Cover Pages** — for the hero on the homepage
- **Instagram Feed block** — to pull in @olivorestaurant content
- **Map block** — for the Contact page
- **Form block** — for Events inquiries and general contact
- **Gallery blocks** — for food and atmosphere photography

### What Squarespace Does NOT Do Well
- Complex online ordering (recommend linking out to a dedicated platform)
- Real-time menu updates from a POS (menu must be manually updated)
- Highly custom animations or interactions
